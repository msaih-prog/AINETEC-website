How to submit

Submission Process

Three steps from draft to review.

STEP 01

Prepare Your Paper

Format your paper using the AINTEC 2027 proceedings template. Papers must be written in English and should not exceed 12 pages including references.

STEP 02

Submit Your Manuscript

Submit your paper through the AINTEC 2027 submission platform. Create an account if you don't have one and follow the instructions carefully.

STEP 03

Peer Review

Your paper will be reviewed by at least two independent reviewers. Authors will be notified of the decision by April 15, 2027.

Instructions

Submission Guidelines

Paper Requirements

  • Papers must be written in English
  • Maximum 12 pages including references
  • Must follow the AINTEC 2027 proceedings format
  • PDF format only for initial submission
  • Anonymous review — remove author information
  • Original and unpublished work only

Important Notes

  • At least one author must register for the conference
  • Accepted papers must be presented at the conference
  • Plagiarism will result in immediate rejection
  • Simultaneous submission to other venues is not allowed
  • Camera-ready papers due: May 10, 2027
  • Source files (LaTeX or Word) required after acceptance
