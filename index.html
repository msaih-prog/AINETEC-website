About the host

ISGA Campus Marrakech

AINTEC 2027 is an international conference organized by ISGA Campus Marrakech — Institut Supérieur d'Ingénierie & des Affaires, member of the Edvantis Group, Marrakech, Morocco.

This conference brings together renowned researchers, experts, and professionals in the fields of artificial intelligence, networking, and emerging technologies. Its primary objective is to explore the latest advances in AI, cybersecurity, communication networks, and intelligent systems.

AINTEC 2027 provides an interactive platform for participants to share ideas, present research findings, and exchange experiences. The conference welcomes researchers, academics, engineers, industry professionals, and students from around the world.

With a strong emphasis on networking, collaboration, and knowledge exchange, AINTEC 2027 represents an excellent opportunity for participants to stay informed about recent developments and emerging trends in artificial intelligence, networking technologies, and multidisciplinary engineering fields.
