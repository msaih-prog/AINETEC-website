About the host

ISGA Campus Marrakech

AINTEC 2027 is organized in Marrakech, at the foot of the High Atlas.

ISGA — Institut Supérieur d'Ingénierie & des Affaires is a State-recognized higher education institution specialized in engineering and management, and a member of the Edvantis Group.

With more than 40 years of experience, ISGA has built a teaching model around hybrid, professionalizing pedagogy — combining academic rigor with direct exposure to industry practice, a philosophy reflected in the AINTEC 2027 program.

AINTEC 2027 brings together researchers, engineers and industry practitioners working across artificial intelligence, networking, and the emerging technologies reshaping both fields.
