Fees, payment details and terms for AINTEC 2027.

Registration Fees

Early bird pricing ends May 1, 2027. Regular pricing runs through the registration deadline, May 10, 2027.

CategoryEarly BirdRegular
PhD Students (Moroccan) 1700 MAD170 € 2000 MAD200 €
PhD Students (International) 2000 MAD200 € 2200 MAD220 €
Professors and Researchers 2200 MAD220 € 2500 MAD250 €
Industrials 3000 MAD300 € 3200 MAD320 €
Non-Author Listener (Attendee) 800 MAD80 € 950 MAD95 €

* MAD: Moroccan Dirhams.

Terms & Conditions

  1. At least one author per paper must register for the conference to present their work.
  2. The registration fee includes access to all conference sessions, daily lunch, tea breaks, and a conference kit.
  3. Accommodation is not included in the registration fee.
  4. Cancellations made before May 1, 2027 will receive a 50% refund. No refunds after this date.
