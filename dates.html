Important Dates

AINTEC 2027 welcomes original and high-quality research contributions. Submitted manuscripts must not have been previously published, accepted for publication, or be under review by any other conference, journal, or workshop at the time of submission. Compliance with these policies ensures the integrity of the peer-review process and the quality of the conference proceedings. Please refer to the important dates below for all submission deadlines and conference milestones.
